Specialised Treatments

Deep Tissue Massage

Designed to alleviate severe tension and pain, this therapy penetrates deep into the muscles, promoting healing and flexibility. Its therapeutic benefits extend beyond relaxation, aiding in recovery from injuries and improving overall physical function. Embrace this technique to restore your body’s balance.

Reflexology

This ancient practice focuses on pressure points in the feet and hands that correspond to different body systems. By stimulating these points, reflexology encourages relaxation and promotes better health throughout the body. Experience a holistic treatment that connects physical and emotional well-being.

Indian Head Massage

This unique therapy targets the scalp, neck, and shoulders, releasing tension and revitalising the mind. Ideal for relieving headaches and enhancing mental clarity, it offers a deep sense of relaxation. Allow yourself to recharge as the stresses of the day melt away under Bali’s skilled hands.

Deep abdominal massage

Deep abdominal massage is a profoundly therapeutic technique specifically designed to promote healing and balance in the core of your body. This specialised form of massage focuses on the abdominal region, addressing both the physical and energetic aspects of well-being.
